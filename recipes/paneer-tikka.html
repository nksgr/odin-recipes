Paneer Tikka

Description

Delicious Indian Paneer Tikka at home. It is so flavorful and easy to prepare. Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer. It has chunks of paneer, crunchy sweet peppers, and onions marinated in yogurt and warm spices and cooked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Yogurt
  • Chilli Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Chat masala
  • Kasoori Methi
  • Chilli flakes
  • Lemon juice
  • Onion (Cut into cubes)
  • Bell peppers (Cut into cubes)
  • Salt

Procedure

    Marinade

  1. Mix together yogurt, dry spices, and lemon juice.
  2. Add cubed paneer, onions and bell peppers into the marinade

    3. Tandoori

  3. Thread into skewers & Air Fry at 360°F (180° C) for 8 minutes
  4. Serve them with Pudina Chutney
